How to make a mug cake


INGREDIENTS:
  • 4 tbsp self-raising flour
  • 4 tbsp caster sugar
  • 2 tbsp cocoa powder
  • 1 medium egg
  • 3 tbsp milk
  • 3 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract (or peppermint)
  • (Optional: chocolate chips, raisins, fruits etc.)

PROCEDURES:

STEP 1:
  1. Add 4 tbsp of self-raising flour
  2. Add 4 tbsp of caster sugar
  3. 2tbsp of cocoa powder

Note: Place ingredients in a big mug to avoid overflowing in the microwave

STEP 2:
  1. Add 1 medium egg
  2. Mix well

STEP 3:
  1. Add 3 tbsp of milk/li>
  2. Add 3 tbsp of vegetable oil
  3. Add 1 tsp of vanilla or peppermint extract
  4. Mix ingredients well
  5. (Optional: Add your chocolate chips, fuits, raisins etc.)

STEP 4:
  1. Place mug in the microwave
  2. Set timer to 1 1/2 mins. - 2mins.
  3. Wait for it to cool down
  4. (Optional: Add whipped cream)

Enjoy your mug cake!
